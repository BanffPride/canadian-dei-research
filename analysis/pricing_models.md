# Pricing Models

Five tiers from subsidized (<$2K/day) to premium ($15-40K+/day).

| Tier | Day Rate | Provider Type |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Subsidized | <$2K | Nonprofits |
| Low | $2-4K | Small trainers |
| Mid | $4-8K | Boutique |
| Mid-Premium | $8-15K | Specialized |
| Premium | $15-40K+ | Big Four |

SaaS disruption: $5-50/employee/year models challenging consulting economics.
