# Executive Summary

80 organizations analyzed across 13 segments and 24 service categories.

**Market Size**: $1.5B-$2.5B estimated annual spend.

## Top 5 Findings

1. **Measurement Gap**: 15% of firms offer DEI analytics — the #1 unmet need
2. **Commoditization**: Unconscious bias training (73% of firms) is oversaturated
3. **Tech Disruption**: SaaS platforms challenging traditional consulting
4. **Frontier Markets**: Neurodiversity (5 providers), AI Ethics (4), Health Equity (6)
5. **Consolidation**: Market expected to concentrate from 80+ to ~35 players by 2035

## Growth: 8-12% CAGR; DEI Tech and Health/Wellness at 15-25%.

## Recommendations

- Investors: Target DEI tech and health equity specialists
- Boutique Firms: Build measurement capability
- Enterprise: Embed DEI in transformation and build proprietary data
- Nonprofits: Add technology to membership models
